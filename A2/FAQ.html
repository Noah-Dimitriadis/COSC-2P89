  • What is your favourite record ACTUALLY though?

    • Faces - Tri Color Edition
    • So yes Faces is probably my favourite rap album of all time, I got the very nice and very fancy tri color edition for my birthday (Love You EM!) two years ago. It was a limited release put out by Mac Millers estate to celebrate the 10 year aniversary of the Faces mixtape along with its release to streaming services. It came with a couple posters and a very fancy tracklist/producer credits card which I will probably frame at some point (another thing on my todo list lol).
